Coffee

I’ve got a coffee hobby. It’s big enough to have its own section.

Snobbery

I’ve been asked if I’m a coffee snob by a lot of people. I don’t think I am. I’ll drink any cup of coffee that is put in front of me. I’ve had my fair share of vending machine coffee and even know preset codes to get what I want. My Starbucks account usually has some money in it for an emergency cup. Strong. Weak. Cheap. Whatever it is, I really don’t mind.

What makes it look like I’m a coffee snob is that I will buy expensive coffee beans and have a decent pour-over setup for my house. I’ll talk about it if you bring it up. It’s my hobby and no different than other types of hobbies.
